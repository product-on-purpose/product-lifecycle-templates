About this map. Generated from the project's 205-type master catalog. Tier 1 is the must-have core (labelled "must-have 28" in the catalog; the enumerated list resolves to 27 distinct canonical types, not fabricated up to 28). Tier 3 flags artifacts whose methodology names a regulatory frame (FDA, ISO, SOC 2, GDPR, and similar). Most Tier 2 and Tier 3 assignments are inferred from the catalog's formality and methodology fields rather than stated per type; a type's drawer notes when its tier was inferred.

Data honesty. The stage shown here is the catalog's own document-stage dimension, normalized to the first segment of a compound field, so a few tokens are really disciplines (architecture, security, enablement) rather than pure stages. It is deliberately NOT the same thing as a bundle's lifecycle phase (the six-value discover/define/develop/deliver/measure/iterate axis this library shares with pm-skills); this field was renamed from phase to stage so the two never get confused (finding TX-2). Rarity carries four source values (common, occasional, rare, rare-or-legacy) preserved verbatim. Relationships were extracted from prose and may not all resolve to another named artifact in the set.
