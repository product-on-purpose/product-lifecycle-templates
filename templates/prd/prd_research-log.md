## Notes and limitations

- SVPG pages and the Hustle Badger article returned HTTP 403 to automated fetch; their substance was
  captured from search excerpts and corroborated against the master catalog (which independently cites
  the same Cagan position). Quotes attributed to Cagan use wording confirmed across both the search
  excerpt and the catalog.
- Lenny's full post body is paywalled; only the public summary was used, so claims from [7] are kept to
  the problem-first and one-pager points that the summary states plainly.
- Vendor sources (9, 10, 11) are used for convention and modern-section consensus, not for contested
  claims of fact, per the citation standard.
- Recency flag: the Cagan position is stable (2007 to 2024); the agile "living document" trend is
  well-established. No time-bound regulatory claims appear in this bundle.
