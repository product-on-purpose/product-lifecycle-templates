## Notes and limitations

- The Cohn quote "every user story is a placeholder for a future conversation" and the Three C's
  definitions were confirmed directly from Mountain Goat Software.
- Jeffries' 2001 Three C's and Wake's 2003 INVEST were corroborated across the Wikipedia history and
  multiple practitioner summaries; primary URLs are cited but were not individually fetched.
- Job stories: Intercom is credited with the invention and Klement with naming and explaining it; both
  the Intercom blog and Klement's jtbd.info post support this.
- No time-bound regulatory claims appear in this bundle.
